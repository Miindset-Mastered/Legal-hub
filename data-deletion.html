Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: November 2025

Under data protection laws, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data associated with Miindset Mastered.

1. Automatic Deletion

If you delete your Miindset Mastered account through the app (or Google account removal), all personal data stored in Firebase — including goals, planner entries, and profile — will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

2. Manual Request

If you’d like to request deletion manually, email us at miindsetmastered@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”.

Include your registered email address so we can verify and remove your account data securely.

3. What Gets Deleted

  • Your user account and profile
  • Your planner, journal, and challenge data
  • All cloud-stored app activity and uploads

4. What Is Retained

We may retain minimal non-personal logs (e.g. anonymized analytics) for app performance and legal compliance purposes.

For questions, contact miindsetmastered@gmail.com
