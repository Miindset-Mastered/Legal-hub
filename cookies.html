1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device to remember preferences, improve performance, and analyze how users interact with the app.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • To remember login sessions
  • To measure app usage (via Firebase Analytics)

3. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services like Firebase and Google AdMob may also use cookies or similar identifiers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your device or browser settings, but some features may not function correctly.
