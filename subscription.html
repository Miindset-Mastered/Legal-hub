Mastered+ Subscription Terms

Last updated: November 2025

1. Overview

Mastered+ is Miindset Mastered’s premium membership, offering extra features including:

  • Ad-free experience
  • Access to Gym and Meal Planners
  • Discounted downloads and exclusive packs
  • Early access to community events

2. Billing

Payments are handled securely through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store, depending on your device.

By subscribing, you authorize the app store to charge your selected payment method on a recurring basis until canceled.

3. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription anytime in your Play Store or App Store account settings. Canceling prevents future charges but does not refund past payments.

4. Renewals

Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle unless canceled at least 24 hours before renewal.

5. Refunds

Refunds are processed directly through the app store’s refund policy. Miindset Mastered cannot issue refunds manually.

6. Changes to Pricing

We may modify pricing or features with prior notice. Any price changes will apply only to future billing cycles.
